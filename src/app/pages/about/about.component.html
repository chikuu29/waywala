About us

The platform is highly valuable for farmers and other individuals alike. For farmers, it aids in growing healthy crops with the help of a crop doctor and provides agricultural inputs such as fertilizers, pesticides, herbicides, seeds, etc. at their doorstep as directed by the crop doctor. Additionally, farmers can sell their products directly to customers through the platform. The platform also provides valuable agricultural information, including the latest market status of crops for increased profitability, weather forecasting up to 5 days to avoid crop loss, news updates on agriculture, KVK details of the nearest KVK for information about various schemes and subsidies, MSP prices, and more.

Moreover, the platform provides e-commerce facilities for quality products, including fruits and vegetables, groceries, electronics, fashion and beauty items, mobile phones, books and educational materials, toys, pharmacy items, furniture and appliances, sports equipment, automotive products, and more, delivered directly to customers' doorsteps. The platform also offers wholesalers and retailers the opportunity to sell their products in a digital format for higher profits.

In addition to agriculture and e-commerce, the platform serves as a learning platform, offering various lectures and study materials from experienced teachers to enhance students' careers with proper guidance.
